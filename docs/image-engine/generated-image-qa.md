## Image Style Policy

Approved style:

- Clean Korean business automation diagrams
- Light background
- Teal and navy accents
- Simple cards, arrows, flow lines, and dashboard-like shapes
- Minimal or no in-image text
- Captions and alt text outside the image when possible

Disallowed content:

- Real logos or branded platform marks
- Copyrighted icons
- People or faces
- Product imagery, Amazon-like ecommerce imagery, affiliate visuals, review visuals, or shopping visuals
- Private company, customer, contract, payment, or sales data
- Fake screenshots of private dashboards
- Unreadable Korean text inside images

## Manual Visual Review Checklist

Before activating any generated image:

- Confirm the image explains the article topic.
- Confirm there are no real logos, people, product shots, or private data.
- Confirm any text inside the image is minimal and readable on mobile.
- Confirm the raw source is local-generated.
- Confirm the optimized WebP loads from `/images/posts/*.webp`.
- Confirm the caption and alt text are Korean and useful.
- Confirm the image does not create horizontal overflow at 320px.
- Confirm `npm run validate:images` passes.
