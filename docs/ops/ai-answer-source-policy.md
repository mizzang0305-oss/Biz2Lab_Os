# Biz2Lab AI Answer Source Policy

Biz2Lab content should be easy for search engines and answer engines to summarize without exaggeration. This policy is for structural clarity, not keyword stuffing or fake AI optimization.

## Good Answer-Source Structure

Use these elements where they naturally fit the article:

- A direct answer in the first few lines.
- A `## 먼저 결론` section when the article answers a decision question.
- A short checklist for adoption, avoidance, or risk review.
- A comparison table when the search intent is comparison or caution.
- A `쓰면 좋은 경우 / 피해야 할 경우` judgment block for tool reviews.
- FAQ items with short, practical answers.
- Related article links with descriptive anchor text.

## Required Tone

- Say what the tool or method is good for.
- Say what it is not good for.
- Name the first operational risk to check.
- Keep claims cautious when licenses, self-hosting, cost, or commercial use are involved.
- Prefer practical judgment over promotional language.

## Forbidden Patterns

- Fake analytics, fake impressions, fake CTR, fake rankings, or fake query lists.
- `meta keywords`.
- Keyword stuffing.
- "무조건 추천"
- "완전 무료"
- "상업 사용 보장"
- Fearmongering or loss framing that overstates risk.
- Legal, tax, or license advice tone.
- Removing self-hosting, license, cost, maintenance, or operations cautions.

## FAQ Rules

FAQ answers should:

- answer the question in the first sentence
- include a practical caveat when relevant
- avoid guarantees
- avoid blind recommendations
- link to related Biz2Lab articles only when the link helps the reader decide

Example:

```text
Q. Flowise는 어떤 경우에 쓰면 좋나요?
A. RAG, 도구 호출, 승인 단계가 있는 AI 에이전트 워크플로를 시각적으로 만들고 싶을 때 적합합니다. 다만 운영 부담과 유지보수 기준을 먼저 정해야 합니다.
```

## Dashboard Rules

The SEO ops dashboard may show:

- AI answer readiness status
- FAQ present status
- conclusion-first status
- checklist status
- comparison-table status
- citation-friendly summary status
- disconnected analytics empty states
- manual Search Console / Naver verification status

The dashboard must not show invented analytics metrics before a real connector is configured.
